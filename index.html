Introduction

2016-11-26 · 113 words · 1 minute read

There is a lot of misinformation floating around on the internet, including misinformation about various aspects of science and technology. From eminent scientists and science popularizers like Prof. Richard Dawkins, Dr. Neil deGrasse Tyson, Prof. Lawrence Krauss and Prof. Brian Cox to an army of internet activists for science like SciBabe, BiologyBabe, A Science Enthusiast and many more, a lot of scientists and engineers are speaking out against pseudoscience. As not only a student of science but also a big fan of the scientific enterprise, I would like to add my two bits to the spread of awareness and combating the deluge of pseudo-science online.
